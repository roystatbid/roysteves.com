What are you doing here?

It's a valid question, after all. You came to my personal website, then clicked the About page. But you wouldn't have come to this domain if you didn't already have a pretty good idea of who the heck I am.

Are you trying to untangle which "Roy Steves" this is? There aren't a lot of us, as neither surname nor given name are especially common, so there's a pretty good chance this does the trick: I'm the Ecommerce geek, not the (frankly ridiculously talented) mural painter.

Are you looking to learn more about me, as a person? Not to deflect, but my LinkedIn profile has a lot more of the archeological depth than you're probably used to.

Are you curious about what I'm curious about? Well, then! Come on in, take a seat, friend.

I wonder how things work, where they came from, and where they might go next. I like to consider what might be possible, unlocked by technologies and realities currently available, but not yet realized. I would wager that the prerequisites for the future have been met, we're just not done implementing it.

Can I get you something? I wasn't expecting guests, but I'd love for you to stick around and chat.
