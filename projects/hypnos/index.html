Channels
Each channel plays a sine wave at a harmonic of the root note. "Root" is the fundamental; higher harmonics add richness and texture.
Volume
Base loudness of each channel. Drag to mix the harmonic balance.
Amplitude (wobble)
Rate — how fast the volume pulses. Depth — how dramatic the pulsing is. Both at zero = steady tone.
Pan (stereo sweep)
Rate — how fast sound moves between speakers. Depth — how wide the sweep is. Creates spatial movement.
Sleep
Fades to silence over 30 minutes, then stops. Tap again to cancel.
Mood
Fullscreen color wash that responds to the audio. Tap or press Escape to exit.
